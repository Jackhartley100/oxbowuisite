FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our platform.

What is Superflo?
Superflo is a powerful workflow automation platform designed to streamline your business processes and boost productivity.
How can Superflo benefit my business?
Superflo helps you automate repetitive tasks, reduce errors, and save time, allowing your team to focus on high-value activities.
Is Superflo suitable for small businesses?
Absolutely! Superflo is scalable and adaptable to businesses of all sizes, from startups to large enterprises.
What kind of integrations does Superflo offer?
Superflo integrates with popular tools like Slack, Trello, Google Workspace, and many more. We're constantly adding new integrations.
How secure is my data with Superflo?
We take security seriously. Superflo uses bank-level encryption and complies with GDPR and other data protection regulations.
Do I need coding skills to use Superflo?
Not at all! Superflo features a user-friendly, drag-and-drop interface that requires no coding knowledge.
What kind of customer support does Superflo provide?
We offer 24/7 email support, live chat during business hours, and comprehensive documentation to help you get the most out of Superflo.
Can I try Superflo before purchasing?
Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required to start.
How often does Superflo release updates?
We push minor updates weekly and major feature releases monthly. All updates are automatically available to our users.
Is there a limit to how many workflows I can create?
Our plans offer different workflow limits. Enterprise plans have unlimited workflows. Check our pricing page for details.
